Grandma's Favorite Apple Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1 package (14.1 oz) refrigerated pie crusts, softened as directed

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix apples and lemon juice. In another small bowl, stir together sugar, brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Sprinkle this mixture over the apples and toss to coat.
  3. Roll out the pie crust and place it in a 9-inch pie dish. Fill with the apple mixture and dot with butter.
  4. Cover with the top crust, seal edges, and cut slits for steam to escape. Brush the top with a little milk for a golden color.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until the crust is golden and the filling is bubbling. Let cool before serving.

Notes

For extra flavor, try adding a pinch of cloves or cardamom to the filling. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream for a truly indulgent treat!
